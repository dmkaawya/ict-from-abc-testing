About Us

Empowering Sri Lankan Students with ICT Knowledge

ICT From ABC is Sri Lanka's leading online ICT education platform, dedicated to helping students master Information & Communication Technology from the very basics to advanced levels.

Founded with a mission to make quality ICT education accessible to every Sri Lankan student, we provide comprehensive resources for O/L and A/L ICT in both Sinhala and English mediums. Our expert team of educators designs every lesson to be simple, engaging, and effective.

Complete O/L & A/L syllabus coverage in Sinhala & English
Step-by-step video tutorials with practical examples
Free past papers, model papers, and study materials
Expert teachers with years of ICT teaching experience
